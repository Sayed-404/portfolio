About Me

I’m Sayed Mohamed Sayed, an Oracle Cloud Infrastructure Engineer with hands-on experience in designing, deploying, and managing cloud solutions across compute, storage, networking, and security domains on Oracle Cloud (OCI). Currently, I’m working at Zone Trading Company, where I build and maintain secure and scalable cloud infrastructures, integrating hybrid data center solutions and applying Oracle’s best security practices. I hold a B.Sc. in Computers and Information from Assiut University (Class of 2024), where I achieved an A+ for my graduation project — an AI-Based Human Resource Management System. I’m passionate about cloud architecture, automation, and cybersecurity, and I continuously develop my expertise through certifications such as OCI Architect Associate, OCI Networking Professional, and Security. Beyond the tech side, I value teamwork, innovation, and continuous learning — always striving to deliver reliable, efficient, and secure cloud environments.
